## Requirements Before Publication

Before this statement may be published in any form, the following must exist and be documented:

1. A formally designated WorkJax operator (see `docs/README.md` and `docs/operations/accessibility.md`)
2. A named accessibility owner accepted in writing by that operator
3. A real, monitored feedback contact
4. A defined response and escalation timeline
5. A defined assessment method and reviewed scope
6. A completed, documented WCAG 2.2 AA evaluation for that scope (see `docs/accessibility/wcag-2.2-aa-checklist.md` and `docs/accessibility/accessibility-audit-log.md`)
7. An accurate conformance status using only the terminology in `docs/operations/accessibility.md` Section 8

No email address, phone number, operator name, response time, or legal entity has been invented for this draft. Every contact and ownership field above is intentionally left as a placeholder.
