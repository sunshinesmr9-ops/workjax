Jax Third Spaces

These aren't one-time events, they're recurring hangouts that happen every week whether you RSVP or not. No ticket, no agenda, just a standing reason to show up.

The Block
Riverside's open-air food truck and bar lot, a rotating lineup of local vendors and a default weeknight hangout.
$3 Thursdays at Happy Medium
The book cafe's weekly deal night, easy to post up, browse, and talk to whoever's at the next table.
Cafe San Marco Jazz Night
Live jazz every Friday, 7:00-10:00 PM, the closest thing in town to the old Jax jazz-hall nights.
Yoga at Riverside Arts Market
Free 9 AM yoga on the lawn before the market opens, bring a mat and stay for the rest of the morning.
Riverside Arts Market
Every Saturday under the Fuller Warren Bridge, 150+ local makers, live music, and food, the easiest first hang.
Arlington Community Garden
BEAM's community garden welcomes volunteers for planting and harvest days, hands in the dirt, low pressure.
Book Clubs
Independent bookstores and cafes around town run rotating monthly clubs, a built-in reason to show up again.
Trivia Nights
Bars across Riverside, San Marco, and downtown run weekly trivia, an easy standing plan with a built-in group.
